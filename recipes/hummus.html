Teta's Hummus

Description

Hummus is a creamy middle eastern chickpea based dip. It is a vegan dip packed with protein from the chickpeas and healthy omega fatty acids from the olive oil.

The health benefits are many, and it's also a snap to make. I think Teta's way tastes better, hand-mashed with a fork and a shallow bowl, but it's super easy to whip up in a blender or a food pro.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas, cooked
  • Tahini
  • High quality extra virgin olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic, peeled and mashed
  • Sea salt, fine
  • Cumin, ground
  • Water

Steps

  1. Add chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and half of the olive oil into a shallow bowl and mash together with a fork.
    2. As a dry paste begins to form, add the rest of the olive oil.
  2. Continue mashing and slowly adding water to achieve a smooth and creamy consistency.
  3. Finish by adding cumin and salt to taste.
